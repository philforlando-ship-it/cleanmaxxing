## Variant notes

**Warm-lead version:** open with "following up on our conversation about…" or specific context.

**Cold version:** open with one line acknowledging their work. One line, not a paragraph. Something like "I've been following your [specific thing] for a while and think your audience is exactly who this is built for."

**Shorter DM version:** cut everything except the first line, the "short version" paragraph compressed to one sentence, and the ask. ~80 words. Use for platforms where long messages get ignored.
